Preston O'Connor

Preston O'Connor, a Computer Science and Math student at the University of Massachusetts-Amherst, merges technology and mathematics in his academic journey. This website reflects his love for innovation and education.

As a USPTR-certified tennis coach for over 4 years, Preston blends technical expertise and coaching finesse. His tenure at the Shrewsbury Club until 2024 involved coaching and hands-on work in the pro shop, where he discovered the need for a user-friendly approach to racket services.

Driven by this realization, Preston created a platform to bridge the gap for tennis enthusiasts interested in racket customization. Dedicated to simplifying the learning process, he offers resources and guidance for those exploring the intricacies of racket stringing and modification.
